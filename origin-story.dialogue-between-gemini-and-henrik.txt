The Action: During the next compaction, the storage engine "shreds" that org_id out of the blob and promotes it to a Primary Index Column.

The Result: The data is physically stored as a narrow, fast lookup table, but the original blob remains in the "Payload" (cold storage). The "Relationship" is now physically optimized for the hardware without the human ever defining a schema.
